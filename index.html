Travel Tips

How to Plan the Perfect Beach Holiday

From choosing the right destination to packing smart—everything you need to know for a sun-soaked escape.

A beach holiday offers sunshine, salt air, and relaxation — but good planning ensures you get the most out of it.

Choose the right destination. Pick a beach that fits your needs (family-friendly vs adventure vs quiet). Look up amenities, crowd levels, seasonality.

Book accommodation early. Popular beach spots fill fast — booking ahead often gets better rates and better rooms.

Plan your activities. Decide whether you’ll relax, snorkel, surf, explore. Pack accordingly.

Packing essentials. Don’t overlook sun-protection (high-SPF sunscreen, hats, sunglasses), quick-dry towels, insect repellent, etc.

Choose the right season & shoulder time. Avoid peak crowding and high prices by picking less busy seasons where possible.

With a little thought ahead (destination + timing + packing), your beach holiday becomes not just relaxing but memorable.

Top Hill Stations to Visit in India

Chill out in the mountains: discover scenic towns, best seasons, and travel tips for your getaway.

Escape the heat, breathe fresh air, and enjoy scenic mountains. India’s hill stations offer this and more.

Choosing your hill station. Consider travel time, climate, crowd levels, activities.

Examples of great hill stations:

  • Darjeeling (West Bengal) – tea plantations, Himalayan views.
  • Coorg (Karnataka) – misty valleys and spice farms.
  • Manali, Shimla (Himachal Pradesh) – adventure, snow, chill.

Best time to visit & activities. Depends on whether you want summer retreat, monsoon greenery or winter snow — adapt accordingly.

Whether for relaxation or adventure, picking the right hill station with a bit of homework makes all the difference.

Travel Hacks

Smart Ways to Save on Your Flights

Budget friendly travel doesn’t mean missing out. Learn how to find deals, avoid hidden fees and fly smarter.

Flights often take up a big chunk of travel budget — but with savvy tactics you can significantly reduce costs.

Key strategies:

  • Use incognito mode or clear cookies while searching — some sites may raise fares when they detect frequent searches.
  • Be flexible with travel dates — shifting your trip by a day or choosing mid-week can lower fares.
  • Compare fares across currencies, routes, and booking platforms.

Bonus tips:

  • Set price alerts on booking apps so you get notified when fares drop.
  • Consider alternative airports or one-way bookings to unlock better deals.

With planning and a few little hacks, you’ll fly smarter — spend less on airfare, and free up budget for better experiences at your destination.
