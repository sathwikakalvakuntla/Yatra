Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. Here’s how we protect it.
Yatra Online Limited (“Yatra”, “we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our travel-planning services and website. By using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following kinds of personal data:

  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone, address.
  • Identity & Passport Details: Passport number, nationality, date of birth, government IDs.
  • Travel Information: Travel history, itineraries, bookings.
  • Payment Information: Billing and transaction details (we do not store full card data).
  • Other Personal Details: Emergency contacts, additional details needed for tours.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser/device info, pages viewed, and cookies for analytics and site-functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to:

  • Process bookings and travel arrangements efficiently.
  • Communicate with you regarding your bookings, payments, updates, and support.
  • Deliver services and manage your travel itinerary.
  • Improve our services and personalise your experience.
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent; you can opt-out anytime).
  • Comply with legal requirements and protect our rights and security.

3. Sharing of Personal Data

We may share your data with:

  • Airlines, hotels, tour operators and travel-service providers to fulfil your bookings.
  • Payment gateways for secure transaction processing.
  • Trusted vendors (e.g., cloud servers, email/analytics providers) under strict confidentiality.
  • Government authorities or regulatory bodies where required by law.

We do not sell or rent your personal data to unaffiliated third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using encryption and secure servers. While we apply strong safeguards, no system is 100% secure. Please protect your login credentials and notify us of any unauthorized access.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for fulfilling the services, legal compliance, and dispute resolution. Data which is no longer needed is securely deleted or anonymised.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you and request corrections.
  • Request deletion of your data when no longer required.
  • Withdraw consent for optional uses such as marketing.
  • Object to certain processing of your data (where applicable).
  • Receive a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@yatra.com.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us and we will remove it.

8. Compliance with Law

We comply with applicable Indian data protection laws, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and the Information Technology Act. By using our services, you consent to processing of your data in India.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our services after any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.
